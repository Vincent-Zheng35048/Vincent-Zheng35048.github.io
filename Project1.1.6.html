This is my first project at Worcester Technical Highschool that involves me working with the MIT app inventor. With a partner, I created a game where you drag a soccerball into a goal.


User Story: A kid really likes to play soccer and also likes video games. He would like to play a game that is related to soccer. He doesn't want the game to be too difficult or too easy.


List of features in this app:

  • Soccerball Image sprite
  • Goal Image sprite
  • Reset game button
  • Moving goal checkbox to make the goal move while the app runs
  • Point system
  • Shows you your shots left
