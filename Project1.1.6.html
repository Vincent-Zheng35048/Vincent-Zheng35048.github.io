Project 1.1.6 Penalty Shootout

This is my first project at the tech school that involves me working with the MIT app inventor. With a partner, I created a game where you drag a soccerball into a goal.

List of features in this app:

  • Soccerball Image sprite
  • Goal Image sprite
  • Reset game button
  • Moving goal checkbox to make the goal move while the app runs
  • Point system
  • Shows you your shots left
